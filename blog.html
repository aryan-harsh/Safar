2 July 2018

The Journey

Day 0 of the Delhi Trip was the day of journey for half of us and the preparation day for the other half, the Ranchi faction, who'll be boarding a flight to Delhi on July 3rd. Whereas Harsh departed from Patna on 2 July 0:11am , midnight to reach Mirzapur at 5:30 am. From there he and Shashwat went on to Delhi.

Harsh: "Well I was pretty much excited and yes, I had to wake up all night, which I pretty much did, watching FIFA and youtube then. Shashwat came to pick me up at the station. We left for Allahabad noon , for most part of the journey, I dozed off, getting the much needed sleep. At Allahabad we went for some shopping at Civil Lines , and later momos and samosa. Near 8'O clock we went to Allahabad Junction, to board Humsafar at 10:20. The newly started train was a beauty and the ride was pretty comfortable. "

On the other hand , Aryan and Ritik, still at Ranchi completed their packing. Did their final checks and geared up for the next day. Well it was sure gonna be a big big day.
